10 Reasons to Book a Sacred Healing Retreat on Menorca [Spain’s Serene Balearic Island] 2024

SR

By Shivam Ranabhat

Posted On: 25th Sep, 2024

Menorca, a tranquil island in the Mediterranean, beckoned me to its shores. Just a short hop from Spain’s bustling coast, this hidden gem hosted my recent sacred healing retreat. Its serene landscapes, Mediterranean cuisine, and azure waters provided the perfect backdrop for renewal and introspection. Sun-drenched for most of the year, Menorca’s idyllic setting is perfect for those seeking holistic wellness amidst nature’s splendor and UNESECO-protected historic sites. Hola, I’m Dave from BookRetreats. I recently traveled solo to Menorca from the U.S. (Boston to Madrid to Menorca—not bad) to attend an 8-day Sacred Healing Retreat, which has received a slew of 5-star reviews from previous guests. Here’s an overview of my Menorca retreat experience and everything you need to know if you’d like to go next.

What Made This Retreat Special

I’ve been to Spain before and loved it, but I never had a chance to visit Menorca. This retreat took place in September, an ideal time to visit the Balearic Islands. It’s still warm enough to swim while pleasant to hike, sightsee, relax in cafes, and browse markets.
